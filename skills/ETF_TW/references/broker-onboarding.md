# Broker Onboarding

## Core account concepts in Taiwan

### Securities account

This is the brokerage trading account used to place ETF orders.

### Settlement account

This is the linked bank account used for cash settlement.
A beginner should understand that opening the securities side alone is not enough; the settlement flow matters too.

## Typical opening flow

1. Choose a broker.
2. Confirm whether online opening is supported.
3. Prepare required identity documents.
4. Prepare a bank account suitable for settlement.
5. Complete identity verification and account setup.
6. Wait for approval.
7. Confirm trading permissions and funding path.
8. Do not rush into a first order before understanding costs and lot type.

## Documents to prepare

Usually include:

- national ID card
- second ID document if required
- personal bank account details

Always confirm exact requirements with the broker.

## What beginners should compare between brokers

- ease of online opening
- odd-lot support
- recurring-investment support
- fee-discount policy
- app usability
- future API availability, if that matters later

## After account opening

Before first trade:

- confirm linked settlement bank flow
- confirm where cash must sit before order day
- confirm trading hours and lot types
- preview total cost before order submission
- decide whether to start with a small odd-lot or recurring plan
